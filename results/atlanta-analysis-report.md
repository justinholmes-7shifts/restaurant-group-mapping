## Data Quality Insights

### Issues Identified:
1. **72% missing data** in original CSV (359 of 500 rows)
2. **Duplicate entries**: LA Chews appeared 5 times (likely same location)
3. **Major underrepresentation**: Ford Fry has 13+ Atlanta locations but only 1 in dataset
4. **South City Kitchen**: 4 locations but only 1 captured
5. **No address data**: Difficult to distinguish multiple locations of same brand

### Validation Challenges:
- Websites blocked from scraping (had to use web search)
- OpenTable/third-party URLs instead of official websites
- Franchise vs corporate ownership ambiguity
- Recent ownership changes (Velvet Taco 2021, Bone Garden/Vortex 2024)

## Market Analysis

### Restaurant Group Penetration:
- **Multi-concept groups**: 11 groups → ~31+ locations (23%)
- **Multi-location brands**: 8 brands → ~30+ locations (22%)
- **National chains**: 6+ brands → ~10+ locations (7%)
- **Independents**: ~70 restaurants (52%)

### Key Observations:
1. **Fragmented market**: Atlanta restaurant scene dominated by independents (52%)
2. **Quality over quantity**: Focus on Michelin stars, James Beard nominations vs rapid expansion
3. **Multi-brand strategy**: Successful groups operate distinct concepts rather than cookie-cutter locations
4. **Local talent**: Strong chef-driven restaurant culture (VanTrece, Quatrano/Harrison, Richards)
