## Market Insights

### 1. Multi-Concept Sophistication
Miami has a **mature restaurant group landscape** with many operators running multiple distinct brands. This is more sophisticated than most markets.

**Implications:**
- Higher deal values (complex needs)
- Need for corporate-level oversight tools
- Different scheduling templates per concept

### 2. Rapid Expansion Brands
Several groups are in **aggressive growth mode:**
- SKINNY LOUIE (viral smash burger - 10+ locations planned)
- Pura Vida Miami (expanding to NYC, Sarasota)
- CVI.CHE 105 (Peruvian fine-casual expansion)
- Groot Hospitality (expanding to Dallas, Las Vegas)

**Implications:**
- Right timing to implement workforce management
- Need for scalable systems
- Corporate standardization opportunities

### 3. Celebrity/Chef-Driven Groups
**High-Profile Operators:**
- Chef Michael Beltran (Michelin-starred) - Ariete Hospitality
- David Grutman - Groot Hospitality
- Gloria & Emilio Estefan - Estefan Enterprises
- Bad Bunny partnership - Gekko

**Implications:**
- Strong capital backing
- Quality-focused operations
- Likely have IT/operations teams

### 4. Franchise Consolidation
**Multi-Unit Franchisees:**
- Erick Passo (3 Benihana locations)
- Nothing Bundt Cakes (3 Miami locations)
- Anthony's Coal Fired Pizza (South Region)

**Implications:**
- Franchisees need systems that work across brands
- Corporate franchise support is key

## Recommendations for 7shifts

### 1. Lead with Success Stories
**Pitch:** "We work with [similar restaurant group] managing XX locations in [city]"

**Target Groups:**
- Multi-location sushi chains → Reference Sushi Sake if they sign
- Multi-concept fine dining groups → Reference Ariete
- Nightlife + restaurants → Reference Groot

### 2. Segment Your Approach

**For Multi-Concept Operators:**
- Value prop: "One system for all your brands"
- Pain point: "How do you manage 10 different concepts?"
- Features: Multiple templates, brand-level permissions, consolidated reporting

**For Single-Brand Chains:**
- Value prop: "Replicate your best practices across 30+ locations"
- Pain point: "How do you maintain consistency everywhere?"
- Features: Template replication, multi-location scheduling, bulk operations

### 3. Timing is Everything

**Target Rapid-Growth Brands NOW:**
- SKINNY LOUIE (planning 10+ new locations)
- Pura Vida Miami (expanding to new markets)
- CVI.CHE 105 (opening new concepts)

**Why:** Growing brands NEED systems before they scale further

### 4. Leverage Miami's Sophistication

**Miami Insight:** This is a **mature, sophisticated restaurant market**
- More multi-concept operators than typical markets
- Celebrity/chef-driven = well-capitalized
- International influence (Latin America, Europe)

**Sales Angle:** Position 7shifts as enterprise-grade solution for sophisticated operators
