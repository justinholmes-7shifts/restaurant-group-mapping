## Key Insights

### Multi-Concept Operators Dominate

The most sophisticated restaurant groups in Miami operate multiple distinct brands:

- **Ariete Hospitality Group:** 10+ concepts from fine dining to casual
- **Groot Hospitality:** Mix of restaurants and nightlife venues
- **Fire Pit Hospitality:** Brewery-focused concepts

### Geographic Clustering

Several groups concentrate in specific neighborhoods:
- **Wynwood:** MPGR Hospitality, multiple independent concepts
- **Brickell:** Groot Hospitality, Tacombi, Pura Vida
- **Little Havana/Calle Ocho:** Gastronomica Restaurant Group
- **Coconut Grove:** Ariete Hospitality Group dominance

### Franchise vs. Corporate

Identified franchise relationships:
- **Tacombi** - Part of national chain
- **Benihana** - Franchised to Erick Passo (Black Market operator)
- **Sushi Sake** - One location (Sunset) may be franchised to Marin Hospitality

### Celebrity/Chef-Driven Groups

- **Ariete Hospitality:** Michelin-starred Chef Michael Beltran
- **Groot Hospitality:** David Grutman (nightlife entrepreneur)
- **Estefan Enterprises:** Gloria and Emilio Estefan
